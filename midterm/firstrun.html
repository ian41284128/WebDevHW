The Starting Line

The only thing that hurts more than running for the first time is realizing it was my own choice to go running.

The outfit:

  • My only pair of basketball shorts
  • Cotton t-shirt
  • Phone in right hand
  • Waterbottle in left hand

Lessons:

Did not consider where I would put the waterbottle, or that my phone would bounce around way too much to go inside my short pockets.

The first run sucked. It hurt, I was unprepared, and I ran really slow. Still, the feeling of reaching the end and knowing I don't have to run anymore might just be enough to keep me going.


Trusting the process
